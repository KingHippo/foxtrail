About us

The Foxes Trail opened December 6th in the Historic Icehouse Building which dates back to 1874. The newly renovated building offers family dining and a fully stocked bar for a night out with the friends. During the warmer months feel free to dine with us on our outdoor patio which overlooks the Heritage trail head.

We offer a wide range of foods that are sure to please everyone in the family. Whether it is steak, chicken, pork, or fish we have an item on the list that you are sure to love.
